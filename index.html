PIG DICE ROLL RULES:-

  1. This is a dice game
  2. There must be 2 players atleast
  3. player 1 rolls the dice and scores points which will be added together untill he/she scores one.If score is one,the dice is passed to the next player 2 and all the points gets lost unless you hold it
  4. when player 2 score is one he/she looses all the scores except the ones that he/she had put on hold.
  5. when the scores on the hold exceeds or equal to 100,the player becomes the winner and there will be an alert onthe screen top
  6. Then the game ends!!!
